Grilled Cheese Recipe

With the recipe down below, you will have no issue wipping up a snack for the kids in the matter of minutes. A classic meal that can be paired with a piping hot bowl of tomato soup, will be sure to satisfy your hunger The best part about the grilled cheese sandwich is that it can be made in anyway you want. There is no limits to what you may want to put inside, but for the sake of a simple recipe, we will just stick to the basics.

List of Ingridents

  • 2 slices bread of your choice
  • 3 slices cheese of your choice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan with a medium-high heat
  2. Assemble your grilled cheese by putting your cheese between both slices of bread
  3. Pour the garlic powder onto the cheese before cooking
  4. Spread the butter on both of the outsides of the bread
  5. Put your sandwich down on the pan and let it toast till satisfaction
  6. When ready, flip the grilled cheese and repeat Step 5
  7. When the cheese is completely melted, remove from pan and let rest
  8. Cut grilled cheese in half diagonally and serve
